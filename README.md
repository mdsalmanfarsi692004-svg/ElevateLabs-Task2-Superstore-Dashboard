## üß† Interview Questions & Answers
*Reflecting on the theoretical aspects of data visualization as requested in the task:*

**1. What is the importance of data visualization?**
Data visualization converts raw data into visual contexts like graphs and maps, making it easier to identify patterns, trends, and outliers that might go unnoticed in text-based data.

**2. When do you use a pie chart vs bar chart?**
Pie charts are best for showing part-to-whole relationships with few categories (e.g., Segment Analysis). Bar charts are better for comparing changes over time or differences between multiple categories (e.g., Sales by Sub-Category).

**3. How do you make visualizations more engaging?**
By using a consistent color theme, minimizing clutter, adding interactive filters (slicers), and focusing on storytelling‚Äîanswering "why" something happened, not just "what" happened.

**4. What is data storytelling?**
It is the practice of building a narrative around a set of data and its accompanying visualizations to help the audience understand the context and take action.

**5. How do you avoid misleading visualizations?**
I ensure axes start at zero where appropriate, use consistent scales, avoid excessive 3D effects that distort proportions, and choose the right chart type for the data.
